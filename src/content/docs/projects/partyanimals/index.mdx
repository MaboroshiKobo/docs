**PartyAnimals** is a modular **player engagement plugin** designed to reward your community through **voting** and interactive, **loot-filled Pinatas**!

This plugin is ideal for servers looking to boost engagement, reward voters, and automate excitement with **community-driven events**.

### Features

This plugin is packed with features designed to bring joy to your community. Check out some of its features:

* Create unique **interactive Pinata** templates with custom health and loot tables.
* Save specific locations as **spawn points** or build excitement by starting a **countdown** before the party begins.
* Optimized entity handling ensures your events remain **performance friendly** and smooth.
* Seamlessly **track votes** via Votifier and reward players, even if they are currently **offline**.
* Automatically trigger **commands** when the server reaches a specific **community vote goal**.
* Track and display your **top voters** using built-in placeholders for Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and All-Time rankings.
* Robust data handling with built-in support for **SQLite**, **MySQL**, and **MariaDB** databases.
* Enjoy full **MiniMessage support** for beautiful gradients and styling in all broadcasts and messages.
